Chart description

This blood pressure chart shows your reading of 98over/65 as a cross in the healthy blood pressure area.

The vertical line (Y-axis) shows systolic blood pressure numbers. The horizontal line (X-axis) shows diastolic blood pressure numbers.

The chart is divided into 4 areas.

  • Low blood pressure is shown in purple. It ranges from 80over/65 to 89over/84.
  • Healthy blood pressure is shown in green. It ranges from 90over/65 to 120over/84.
  • Slightly raised blood pressure is shown in yellow. It ranges from 121over/65 to 134over/84.
  • High blood pressure is shown in red. It ranges from 135over/85 to 169over/99.

If you feel well and do not have any symptoms, check your blood pressure at a GP surgery or pharmacy at least once every 5 years, or sooner if you’ve been advised to by a healthcare professional.

If you have a monitor at home, you can check your blood pressure more frequently.

If you’re at risk of high blood pressure, for example, if you’re overweight, smoke or are over 65, you should check your blood pressure more frequently.

Record your readings and come back to this tool to get advice on what to do next.
